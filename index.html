About

I'm a Boise-based comedian with 2 years of experience, nearly an hour of material, and regular performances at local shows. My style mixes bold, dark humor with sharp observations that keep audiences engaged and laughing.

Whether it's finding the absurd in everyday situations or diving into the darker corners of life with wit and intelligence, I bring a unique perspective that resonates with diverse audiences. I'm available for comedy clubs, corporate events, private parties, and festivals.
